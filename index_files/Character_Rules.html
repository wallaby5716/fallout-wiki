Level up
  • 10 + INT skill points (XP) per level
  • +1 perk (every other level)
  • (END / 2) HP per level

Assign S.P.E.C.I.A.L. Attribute Points

All SPECIAL attributes start at 5, and you have 5 extra points to spend.
You can move points between attributes, but must have a minimum of 1 in each attribute, and no attribute can go above 10.


Strength

All Melee Damage, slightly influences starting HP
Perception
Your ability to perceive your environment, aids in fine-motor control.
Endurance
Greatly influences HP, ability to use heavy weapons like Big Guns and Super Sledges.
Charisma
Ability to get people to not shoot you on sight.
Intelligence
Technical and general knowledge, as well as number of xp gained on level-up
Agility
Amount of Action Points gained per round, and fine + gross motor skills.
Luck
Determines Gambling skill, your critical hit chance.


Choose Skills + Perks

Choose 3 tag skills (+15)
Choose 1 perk


Starting Health

HP = 25 + STR + (2 * END)


AC

AC goes in the head slot.

DAM RES

DAM RES goes in the chest slot.


AP

Your character's AP each turn is equal to 5 + floor(AGI / 2)
This is calculated/ applied automatically.


RADS

rads start at 0, and are gained by exploring irradiated locations, eating/drinking irradiated food, getting attacked by some enemies, using certain chems, and much, much more!

  • 500 = death
  • RADS impose a penalty on every d100 roll you make.
    • The penalty ranges from 1%-50%, and is calculated like this: floor(RADS / 10)
